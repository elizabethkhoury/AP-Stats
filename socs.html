Describing Data: SOCS

SOCS stands for:

  • Shape: Describe the overall pattern of the data (e.g., symmetric, skewed, uniform, bimodal).
  • Outliers: Note any data points that fall far away from the rest of the data.
  • Center: Identify a typical value or the middle of the data (mean or median).
  • Spread: Describe the variability or range of the data (range, IQR, standard deviation).
