Type I Error

Definition: We reject H0 when H0 is true.

Interpretation: Therefore we believe there is significant evidence to suggest that H0 is not true, when it actually is.

Probability: α (alpha level)

Type II Error

Definition: We fail to reject H0 when Ha is true.

Interpretation: Therefore we do not believe there is significant evidence to suggest that Ha is true, when it actually is.
